# Grant Application (Q04 & Q05)

## 04. What are you building?
MarketSprint AI is an autonomous competitive intelligence agent for product designers. It automates research, analysis, and report generation so designers can make faster product decisions.

## 05. Why now?
Design teams are overloaded by fragmented market data. AI-native workflows now make it feasible to produce high-quality competitor insights in minutes, not days.
